About MeCode

Our team is a perfect balance of thinkers and doers, with members from a diverse range of backgrounds such as engineering, designing, marketing, and much more. We are not your average software company and the services we provide are testament to that.

The operational philosophy at MeCode is a fusion of the unconventionality synonymous with recent generations; and the grit and perseverance prominent in older generations, of whom we revered in our youth. With an eye towards the future, striving to be at the forefront of technology, we do not forget the lessons that the past have to teach.
